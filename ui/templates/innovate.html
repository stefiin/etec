Virtual Transformation of COVID-19 Protocols and Principles

Until quite recently, knowing how to offer services online was optional. But in the rapidly changing world of a global pandemic, the option has become a requirement. Improve professional skills of Practitioners in virtual assessment, consultation and treatment for patients with essential rehabilitation needs according to Rehabilitation Industry COVID-19 Protocol. This training course defines the steps involved from planning, development, implementation, and maintenance.

This training was designed for Chiropractic and Physiotherapy Clinics, Community and Multi-disciplinary Rehabilitation Centres. It defines and automates the three key functions in management practice:

1. Healthcare practice for physiotherapists.

2. Healthcare practice for occupational therapists.

New Business Function in Universal Office 2020

This training was designed as a unique course for assessment centers, physiotherapy, and pain management clinics to help practicing staff cope with these uncertain times. It is a combination of training modules for providers workflows and clinic administration workflows based on Antibex / Universal Office software.

This is training solution for rehabilitation industry to achieve the following goals:

1. Improve professional skills of administration.

2. Training for case managers and practitioners in billing, reporting, and scheduling.

3. Optimize patient management, and other clinic administration workflows, according to Rehabilitation Industry COVID-19 Protocol.

Multi-factorial Strategies of Home Modification

For clients with physical limitations due to injury or chronic illness, home modification is a fundamental component in rehabilitation. Each patient’s case requires a personalized treatment plan that integrates assistive devices in the home. This course emphasizes ergonomic assessment techniques and application methods that will increase clients’ independence and restore the ability to perform activities of daily living. Our training highlights the importance of educating patients on safe usage of specific assistive devices, correct application procedures for patient assistance programs, and equipping care providers with the knowledge needed to develop effective treatment strategies.

Chronic Pain Management and Innovative Treatment Strategies

Chronic pain management poses a unique set of challenges to rehabilitation specialists and requires personalized, innovative treatment strategies. This customizable course is designed for chiropractic and physiotherapy clinics, occupational therapists, and community or multi-disciplinary rehabilitation, medical professionals to develop and improve upon skills relating to assessment, consultation, and treatment for patients living with chronic pain. Health care professionals who complete this course will build upon the following skills:

1. Categorizing patients into accurate care groups.

2. Treatment strategies for specific chronic pain conditions.

3. Health care practices for physiotherapists and occupational therapists.

4. Utilizing knowledge gained from the Neuro-Rumsey Chronic Pain program to deliver multi-faceted treatment strategies.

5. Practical data collection and analysis for research studies.

Game Ready Operation Protocols

Game Ready is a revolutionary treatment system that sets a new standard in recovery for acute musculoskeletal injuries and post-op rehabilitation. Our in-depth operations training for this new technology is essential for medical specialists who want to stay up to date on the latest innovations in treatment methods. This course details best practices for the Game Ready pneumatic compression system along with ways to integrate this technology with traditional forms of physiotherapy and chiropractic rehabilitation. Compression therapy providers who complete our training will gain technical skills and practical knowledge needed to keep your clinic on the cutting-edge.
