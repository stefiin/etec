Services

Our services include, but are not limited to:

Excel Training

Excel is the most powerful tool in the Microsoft Office Suite, yet most employees have little to no formal training. We cater to beginners in technology and advanced Microsoft Office professionals. We cover advanced training including VBA Marcros, Data Analysis and integration with Microsoft Access, Word and Powerpoint. Our knowledgable staff will provide your employees with the confidence to build effective tools.

Excel Basics

Our Excel Basics program is a great starting place for new users. We cover fundamentals such as using functions, creating formaulas, formatting worksheets and visualizing data. Create great habits from the start with formatting standards and best practices.

Advanced Excel

Our Advanced Excel program is perfect for self-taught Excel users wanting to brush up on their skills, or long time Excel professionals looking to learn about new functions. We cover VBA Macros, Pivot Table Analysis, What-If Scenarios and Solver.

Management Training

Your Team is your organizations most invaluable resource. Our Management and Culture Training is beneficial for all teams new and old. We focus on bringing the team together and aligning individual objectives with the organization. Learning to clearly communicate between team members and management is a central theme in all of our programs. We utilize the newest developments in Psychology and Management Theory to train your next generation of leaders.

Leadership

Leadership is a skill that all employees should hone. Everyone from an experienced leader to a new employee can benefit from our training, whether they are leading a hundred person team or a small project. Our program will develop all of the soft skills needed to manage and motivate a group.

Team Building

Our Team Building program helps to bring teams together. We focus on allowing your team to effectively contribute to the organization through improving your ability to collaborate and resolve problems. We serve the needs of the modern cross-functional, global or virtual team.

Policy Training

Policies affect every organization and it is critical to have an understanding of the ones that impact your business. Policies can change frequently, and our program will allow your team to react and adapt to these changes as they occur without disrupting existing processes. In addition, our program covers fundamentals for organizations looking to create or improve upon their IT, privacy or governance policies.

External Policies

External Policies are set in place by governing bodies and can have diverse effects on business if you are not compliant. Learn how to engage funders in policy change and understand political staff in our program.

Internal Policies

Internal Policies are essential for growing businesses to protect your company and customers. Our programs focuses on developing IT and Privacy policies that withstand the changing technological landscape.
